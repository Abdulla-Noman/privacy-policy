Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Abdulla Al Noman ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Flutter Book mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Personal Data

While using our Service, we may ask you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information (Personal Data). This includes:

  • Email address (used for Firebase Authentication)
  • Learning progress and Quiz scores
  • Device information (Model, Android version)

2. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard third-party services to power the educational features of the app:

  • Firebase Authentication: Secure sign-in management.
  • Cloud Firestore: Synchronizing your learning journey and bookmarks.
  • Firebase Messaging (FCM): Sending daily learning reminders and push notifications.
  • Google AI (Gemini): Powering the interactive AI chat and code explanation features.
  • Google ML Kit: On-device processing for Image and Text recognition labs.

3. Data Usage & Analytics

We use Google Analytics for Firebase to understand how users interact with the app. This helps us identify which Flutter widgets or tutorials are most helpful and improve the curriculum.

4. App Permissions

To provide a full learning experience, the app may request access to:

  • Internet: Required to sync progress and load external documentation.
  • Notifications: Used for optional daily reminders to help you stay consistent with your learning.
  • Camera: Used strictly for the "Image Picker" and "ML Kit" educational labs. We do not store your photos on our servers.

5. Local Storage

Flutter Book uses local databases (Hive and Sqflite) to store your preferences and cached code snippets on your device. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the app cache in your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at the email provided below.
